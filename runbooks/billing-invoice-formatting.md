# Billing Invoice Formatting

## Billing Invoice PDF Formatting

<!-- chunkId: runbook-billing-invoice-formatting-001 -->

Invoice PDFs are generated by the billing-service export pipeline. Formatting issues (misaligned totals, missing line items) are almost always caused by a template version mismatch after a billing-service deploy, unrelated to notification, authentication, or database issues.
